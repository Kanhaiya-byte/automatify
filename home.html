Speech Recognition

Welcome to our Speech Recognition tool! Our advanced technology allows you to convert spoken words into text effortlessly. Whether you're transcribing interviews, recording meeting notes, or simply want a convenient way to capture your thoughts, our Speech Recognition feature has you covered.

How it works:

  1. Click the "Start Recording" button to begin capturing your speech.
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone. Our system will process your speech in real-time.
  3. When you're finished, click the "Stop Recording" button.
  4. Your spoken words will be instantly transcribed into text and displayed on the screen.
